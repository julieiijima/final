A quick guide to

thrifting

You might find these few tips to come in handy
when you're starting your thrifting journey.

Go on Wednesday’s.
The weekends are always crowded! During the middle of the week, you are more likely to have good finds with less competition.

Shop out of season.
The best deals are always off-season. Look for summer clothes in the winter and vise versa.

Don't give up.
Don’t get too overwhelmed by the endless pile of clothes! Keep looking until you find something that you fall in love at first sight. Thrifting is a treasure hunt.
